About This Project

This project started because I got tired of forgetting the things I like. We can use this space to list all the things we like and don't want to forget.

When I was a young boy, I always wanted to explore new places and try different foods. This project is a way for me to keep track of all the amazing things I've discovered. I remember when I was at the first thanksgiving with the pilgrims, we all got off of the Mayflower and had a great time playing Street Fighter 2 and watching the David Letterman Show. I can't believe he's been on TV for so long!

My Top 5 Favorite Movies and Video Games

Here are my top 5 favorite movies and video games:

  1. Terminator 2 (Movie)

    Terminator 2 is a movie about how computers have ruined everything. Even right now, I'm typing up this website on a computer instead of eating my sandwich and reading about the tragic endeavors of celebrities. How am I supposed to have time to read People magazine when all this techonology is distracting me. Help me Arnold!

  2. Resident Evil 0 (Video Game)

    Resident Evil 0 is a game about surviving the human instinct to give up when something is too hard. This game is entertaining because it's a survival horror experience where you get to play 2 characters at the same time. This game is dreadful because it's a survival horror experience where you get to play 2 characters at the same time.... Everyone should play this to truly understand total hopelessness when you have to go all the way back to the mansion and get the hookshot.

  3. Toy Story (Movie)

    Toy Story is a film about how a child's imagination is inflicting irreversable trauma on toys. Why are my toys plotting political espionage and asserting agendas with other groups of toys. What have we done?

  4. Telltale's The Walking Dead (Video Game)

    Telltale's The Walking Dead is the most realistic depiction of how people will do the most irrational things when faced with a horde of zombies. Why are we sending a little girl into a zombie infested house by herself. Why is the guy who knows where everything is and leading us to safety also completely unaware of how dumb it sounds to cram 10 people onto a boat and navigate the sea with no instruments. Come on Kenny.

  5. Hell's Kitchen (TV)

    Hell's Kitchen is not a Movie or a Video Game, but that's not important. Have you seen how often people mess up the simplest things? At least 2 times a season Gordon is like "Madam, the stove is not even on. Come on guys. *unquestioningly pondering how he got here*. If there's one thing that I learned from watching this show, it's that I must always know where the lamb sauce is at all times. Behind, Chef!
